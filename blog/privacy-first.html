Privacy-First Design: Your Data Stays Local

Why DiskLens is 100% local, read-only, and will never touch your files or send data anywhere.

Being fast and pretty isn't enough these days. A modern tool has to be built with trust at its core. That means a huge focus on privacy, safety, and features that are actually helpful.

The Core Promise

Your data stays on your computer. Period.

100% Local Processing

DiskLens is completely local. Here's what that means:

  • ✓ No servers - We don't have any servers receiving your data
  • ✓ No network calls - The app works completely offline
  • ✓ No analytics - We don't track how you use the app
  • ✓ No account required - Just download and use

The only network call DiskLens makes is an optional check for updates (which you can skip). Even that only fetches a small JSON file with version info - it never sends anything about your system.

Read-Only By Design

This part is super important.

DiskLens is physically incapable of deleting or changing any of your files. It's totally safe.

The app only reads your file system to gather size information. It never:

  • Deletes files
  • Moves files
  • Renames files
  • Modifies file contents
  • Changes permissions

This means you can scan any drive - even sensitive ones - without worrying about accidental damage. DiskLens is a pure observer.

What About the Cache?

DiskLens does cache scan results locally to speed up re-scans. This cache:

  • Is stored only on your computer
  • Contains only file paths and sizes (no contents)
  • Can be cleared anytime from Settings
  • Is never uploaded anywhere

No Telemetry. Ever.

Many apps today collect "anonymous usage data" to improve their products. We don't.

We DON'T collect:

  • Your file names or paths
  • Your folder structure
  • Your disk size or usage
  • Your computer specs
  • How often you use the app
  • What features you click

What stays on your machine:

  • All scan results
  • Your settings
  • Your theme preferences
  • Cached data
  • Everything. All of it.

Trust Through Transparency

We believe privacy shouldn't require trust - it should be verifiable. That's why:

  • ✦ The app works completely offline (test it yourself)
  • ✦ No login or registration is ever required
  • ✦ The update check is the only network request (and it's optional)
