Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025

Overview

DiskLens is a desktop application that analyzes disk usage on your local computer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

DiskLens does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • All disk scanning happens locally on your computer
  • No file contents are ever read or analyzed
  • No data is sent to our servers or any third parties
  • No analytics or telemetry is collected

Update Checks

DiskLens may periodically check for updates by contacting our website. This request only includes your app version to determine if an update is available. No personal information is transmitted.

Local Storage

DiskLens stores your preferences (theme, settings) locally on your computer. This data never leaves your device.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@disklens.app
